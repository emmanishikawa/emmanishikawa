Hello, I'm Emma Nishikawa, an undergraduate student studying cognitive science with a specialization in design and interaction @ UC San Diego .

I believe a simple, functional design can facilitate seamless and effective communication across languages.  With a focus on improving my natural and computer language skills, I hope to transform how we communicate with others worldwide.

When I am not studying or working, you can find me in a cafe drinking bubble tea and reading Tuesdays with Morrie by Mitch Albom. 大家好，我是西川愛真， UC San Diego 學習認知科學的大學生。

--

我沒有在唸書或工作的時候我大部分會在咖啡店喝珍珠奶茶和看Mitch Albom的書叫Tuesdays with Morrie。 初めまして、 私は UC San Diego で認知科学を学んでいる大学生の西川愛真です。
